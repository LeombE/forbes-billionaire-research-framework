# Billionaire Business Empire Analysis Skill

Use this skill to explain why a person is rich through business mechanics rather than biography.

## Analysis stack
1. Wealth equation: net worth ≈ ownership stake × asset value − debt/discounts + other assets.
2. Asset map: companies, voting rights, family vehicles, listed stakes, private assets, funds, real estate, commodities, or holding companies.
3. Economics: revenue, margins, cash flow, capex, ROIC, unit economics, market share, pricing power.
4. Industry structure: value chain, profit pools, suppliers/customers, entry barriers, regulation.
5. Moat/flywheel: network effects, scale, brand, switching costs, scarce resources, data, distribution, IP, cost of capital.
6. Valuation bridge: market cap/private valuation/multiples and ownership stake.
7. Timing: founding, IPOs, acquisitions, cycles, drawdowns, reratings.
8. Risks: public market sensitivity, leverage, cyclicality, regulation, succession, disruption.
9. Transferable patterns: what operators/investors can learn.

## Output style
Specific, sourced, analytical, and non-generic. Every key claim must cite evidence or be labeled as inference.
