# Data License And Source Policy

## Canonical Data

The canonical annual ranking source is the official Forbes World’s Billionaires annual list for the target year. The project stores source locators, normalized fields, derived summaries, and citations for research purposes.

## Do Not Publish

- Raw paywalled pages, copied profile prose, or proprietary downloaded source content when terms prohibit redistribution.
- Login-protected, bypassed, or access-controlled content.
- Private cookies, API tokens, credentials, or session headers.
- Forbes raw JSON/API caches, populated annual manual-import CSVs, processed Forbes-derived outputs, Excel workbooks, generated charts, enriched evidence registries, or billionaire DOCX reports.

## Acceptable Public Outputs

- Schemas and blank manual-import templates.
- Synthetic sample CSVs using fake people, fake companies, fake URLs, and clear `SYNTHETIC SAMPLE` markers.
- Derived metrics and summaries where permitted.
- Source URLs, source titles, publishers, accessed dates, evidence notes, and limitations.
- Original analysis text with citations and clear data limitations.

## Real-Time Data

Forbes Real-Time Billionaires data is optional and must stay in a separate comparison file. It must not overwrite annual rank, annual net worth, or the annual Top 100 canonical population.

## Manual Import

If official annual source access is blocked, rate-limited, paywalled, or legally unclear, use manual-import templates. Missing fields must stay blank until supported by a source row or source note.
