# Synthetic Samples

These CSVs are fake schema demonstrations for GitHub-safe publication.

- They are not Forbes data.
- They do not contain real billionaires, real companies, or real net worth values.
- They use `https://example.com/...` locators and `SYNTHETIC SAMPLE` notes.
- They are intentionally small and are not valid inputs for a final Top 100 run.

Use the blank templates in `templates/` for private manual import work.
